Top 3 Festivals in Mumbai

Festival Month Description
Ganesh Chaturthi August/September A grand celebration dedicated to Lord Ganesha, with elaborate processions and immersion of Ganesha idols.
Diwali October/November The Festival of Lights, celebrated with fireworks, sweets, and decorations all over the city.
Holi March The Festival of Colors, marked by joyous celebrations with colored powders and water.
